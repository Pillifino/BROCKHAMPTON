BROCKHAMPTON FAN PAGE

Bio

Brockhampton is an American hip hop boy band founded in 2010 as AliveSinceForever in San Marcos, Texas. Led by Kevin Abstract and formed partially through the online forum "KanyeToThe", Brockhampton was a self-described boy band, so-called in an effort to redefine the term.
